Keep Your Baby Snug In Our Nests

BABYBU nests are the perfect accessory to any little ones nursery. Safe for playing, resting and relaxing, the BABYBU provides a comfortable place for baby to rest their head. Available in two colors, there is a baby nest to suit everyones lifestyle. The machine washable covers make for stress free cleaning and the pods allow for easy travelling.

Breathable & Adjustable

Nests have become a baby must have with their multi functionality and portability. A safe napping space for supervised daytime sleeping, tummy time, and play, they recreate a feeling of baby being held so they can settle easier and for longer.The BABYBU nest is entirely breathable with a mesh bases which also helps with ventilation and heat regulation.

Perfect Nest For Newborns

For new-borns, the BABYBU nest creates a cosy space with its safer fully enclosed design. The base is soft but taught so no corners or gaps for tiny fingers and toes. The nest can be used up until 1.5 years old and makes an amazing transition product from cot to bed. The nest is super lightweight so great for travelling, camping and sleep overs.
