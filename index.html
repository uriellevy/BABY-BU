Keep Your Baby Snug In Our Nests

Babynest by Babybu is uses the baby from birth and in the first months of life. The product, opens to a sheet, serves as a shell for the baby, which protects it with soft, pleasant and surrounding walls.This allows the baby to develop better, calmer and safer.The inner walls are designed with geometric illustrations that have been proven to contribute to the baby’s  visual development, as has the use of black and white, which are the colors the baby sees in the first period after birth. Babybu is a light and flexible product that allows easy and simple mobility.  It blends in harmoniously with the environment and wherever it is decided to place it: in the living room, in the garden, in the children's room or in the bedroom.   It is knitted from bamboo and cotton yarns recommended for babies and filled with nanofiber, an hypoallergenic and environmentally friendly material. BABY-BU certified for STANDARD 100 by OEKO-TEX®. BABY-BU Made in Israel.

Breathable & Adjustable

Nests have become a baby must have with their multi functionality and portability. A safe napping space for supervised daytime sleeping, tummy time, and play, they recreate a feeling of baby being held so they can settle easier and for longer.The BABYBU nest is entirely breathable with a mesh bases which also helps with ventilation and heat regulation.

Perfect Nest For Newborns

For new-borns, the BABYBU nest creates a cosy space with its safer fully enclosed design. The base is soft but taught so no corners or gaps for tiny fingers and toes. The nest can be used up until 1.5 years old and makes an amazing transition product from cot to bed. The nest is super lightweight so great for travelling, camping and sleep overs.
